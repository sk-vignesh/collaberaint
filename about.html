Our Company

About Collabera International

Our Identity

A Considered Business Built on Solid Foundations

Collabera International Limited is a UK-registered holding company with active operations in two complementary fields — commercial construction and the wholesale distribution of soft drinks and beverages.

We are a lean, professionally run enterprise that prioritises quality of work, reliability of delivery, and honesty in all our dealings. Our structure allows us to operate independently across both divisions while benefitting from shared leadership, values and financial discipline.

Incorporated in England and Wales and registered with Companies House, we are a fully compliant UK business committed to operating transparently and professionally.

How We Work

Our Core Values

These principles guide every decision we make, every project we take on, and every client relationship we build.

🤝 Integrity

We are straightforward, transparent and honest — with clients, partners and suppliers. We say what we mean and do what we say.

⭐ Quality

Whether it's a construction project or a wholesale delivery, we hold ourselves to a high standard of work. Average is not good enough.

📦 Reliability

Our clients depend on us. We show up, meet deadlines, fulfil orders, and communicate proactively when things change.

🌱 Sustainability

We take a long-term view of business, building relationships that last and working in ways that are responsible and sustainable.

Two Divisions, One Company

Division One

Construction

Our construction division undertakes commercial construction projects across the UK, from new-build offices and retail units to civil works and commercial refurbishments. We offer full project management from feasibility through to handover.
