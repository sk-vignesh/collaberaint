Identity

Who We Are

Collabera International Limited is a UK-registered holding company with active operations across two sectors. We are lean, professionally run, and built around the principle that business should be straightforward — quality work, honest pricing, dependable delivery.

We take the long view. Rather than chasing volume, we build the kind of partnerships that last — with our clients, our suppliers, and the trades and logistics businesses we work alongside.

Incorporated in England and Wales, registered with Companies House, and fully compliant — we operate transparently in everything we do.
