Trading & Distribution

The Trading Division

Connecting Brands to British Business

Collabera International's trading arm operates as a wholesale sourcing and distribution business specialising in soft drinks and non-alcoholic beverages.

We work with producers, importers and brand owners to get their products to market efficiently, and with retailers, caterers and hospitality businesses to ensure they receive reliable, competitively priced stock.
